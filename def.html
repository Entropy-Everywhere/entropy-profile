Definitions

A subsection of Therianathropy that includes fictional/otherwordly creatures.

Someone who is a fictional character in a past life/alternative universe. Usually identifying as a character or species. Not to be confused with an IRL who believes they are a fictional character in the present moment in a delusional sense.

Simular to fictionkin, but you identify WITH a character or species rather than AS a character.

Someone who has a special connection to a animal species or collection of animals on a spiritual, mental, or physical level; In the sense of being that animal/species in a past life or alternative universe.

Someone who ships themselves with a fictional character or collection of fictional characters, often using their own oc in place of themselves or act like they're a real life partner (which is a bit more on the unhealthy side-).

A character one uses to represent themselves.
